Delicious Cookie Recipe!

Like made of Grandma's

GrandMas Choco-Kek

Ingredients:
  • 6 EGGS
  • 2 Cups of flour
  • 1 Cup of chocolate chips
  • 2 Tablespoons of vanilla extract
  • 2 Teaspoons of baking powder
  • 1 Cup of sugar

    • Instructions
    1. Add eggs, flour and vanilla extract and mix
    2. Combine with sugar, chocolate chips and baking powder and stir
    3. Take the cookie batter and with your hand form cookie shape dough
    4. Bake at 250 degree for 30 minutes
    5. Let them cool down for 2 hours
    6. Guten Apetit
